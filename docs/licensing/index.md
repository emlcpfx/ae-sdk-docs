# Licensing & Distribution

Protecting and distributing commercial AE plugins.

## Distribution Platforms

| Platform | Revenue Share | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **aescripts + aeplugins** | ~70/30 split | Largest AE plugin marketplace |
| **Gumroad** | ~5% fee | Direct sales, you handle support |
| **LemonSqueezy** | ~5% fee | Gumroad alternative |
